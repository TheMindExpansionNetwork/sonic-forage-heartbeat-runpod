# Lore · Sonic Forage · Heartbeat

*A field note from the foragers who wired DEMON to RunPod.*

---

## The Forage

Before tracks were files, they were **fields** — unstable, alive, full of edible harmonics. **Sonic Forage** is the practice of walking those fields with your hands on the knobs: you don't freeze the world into a WAV and walk away; you keep **picking** new timbre, new structure, new denoise weather while the engine hums.

## The Heartbeat

Every slice over the wire is a **pulse**. The HUD playhead is the metronome of belief. The kick in the shader is the same kick in your chest when Play finally connects after ninety seconds of GPU prayer. We call that moment **Heartbeat** — proof the forge is listening.

## The RunPod Forge

RunPod is a **portable forge**: two doors (6660 and 1318), a proxy that speaks HTTPS to the outside world, and a GPU that never learned PortAudio. The foragers who came before you left runes in `FIXES.md` so you wouldn't bleed on WebSockets and missing checkpoints.

This edition is maintained by collaborators who care about **live** AI music — not slideshows of waveforms, but rooms where motion and sound co-evolve. Fork it, teach with it, break it, fix it, add your own chapter to `LESSON.md`.
