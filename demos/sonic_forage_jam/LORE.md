# Jam Lab · field notes

**Sonic Forage** hunts sound in the DEMON graph. **AVTR** gives the hunt a face — 25 fps, lip-synced, listening when you’re quiet.

This folder is where we **jam**: motion shaders on 6660, avatar video on 7860, stems switched between **vocals** and **full track** when we want the engine to hear only the voice or the whole storm.

No single binary yet — two doors, one GPU, lots of experiments. Write what worked in `NOTES.md` (your session log).
